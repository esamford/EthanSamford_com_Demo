Ethan Samford

Full-Stack Developer

I’m a full-stack developer knowledgeable in Python, the Django and Flask web frameworks, Amazon Web Services, and MySQL databases. I’m a detail-oriented person with a passion for tackling challenging problems with clear benefits for overcoming them. With nearly four years of customer-service work experience, and over four years of development experience, I am able to quickly identify and understand problems, then offer solutions.

I spent 2016 to 2020 at Pittsburg State University; my major there was in Computer Information Systems, and I learned a bit about back-end development in the form of MySQL databases. Additional C++ and Visual Basic courses helped teach me the foundations of object-oriented programming. In addition to that, I also spent time outside of class teaching myself how to program using both Java and Python.

Between February 2017 and November 2020, I worked as a front desk clerk at my university’s library. This was a customer-service position where I used my interpersonal skills to understand the needs of library patrons so I could most effectively help them. Here I also strengthened my organizational and detail-oriented skills by returning materials after use.

Later in 2021, I enrolled in an online bootcamp called Coding Dojo to learn more about full-stack development. This included front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and jQuery. Back-end technologies included Python, MySQL, Flask and Django, and Amazon Web Services.

After graduating from Coding Dojo in April of 2022, I developed three websites on my own: Shareable Events, my portfolio, and Organize My Route. These were personal projects to demonstrate that I could develop websites, as well as reasons to learn more about subjects I was still unfamiliar with. You can find out more about them on the "Projects" page.
